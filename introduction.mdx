## <Icon icon="sparkles" /> What is Konstantly?

Konstantly is an AI-first learning management platform that revolutionizes how you create, deliver, and track online courses. With our AI Canvas Agent, you can build engaging courses in minutes instead of hours—simply describe what you need, and watch it come to life on the canvas.

Whether you're creating compliance training, onboarding programs, or skill development courses, Konstantly provides everything you need from course authoring to analytics.
