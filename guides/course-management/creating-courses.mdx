## Creating a New Course

This guide will walk you through the process of creating a new course in Konstantly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Course Creation">
    Navigate to the Courses section and click "Create New Course"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Course Details">
    Fill in the course title, description, and other basic information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Course Content">
    Upload your course materials, videos, and resources
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set up completion requirements, certificates, and other course settings
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish Course">
    Review and publish your course to make it available to learners
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Course Settings

Configure various aspects of your course:

- **Completion Requirements**: Set criteria for course completion
- **Certificates**: Enable automatic certificate generation
- **Access Control**: Control who can access the course
- **Drip Content**: Schedule content release over time

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Start with a clear course outline before creating content to ensure logical flow and structure.
</Tip>
